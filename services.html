Program Manager

A program manager is a senior-level professional responsible for coordinating and managing a portfolio of projects that contribute to an overarching business objective. Unlike project managers who focus on individual projects, program managers look at the big picture, ensuring all projects within a program work together to achieve strategic outcomes.

Tax Management

A tax manager ensures compliance with tax laws, minimizes liabilities, and optimizes tax strategies, making them a key player in financial planning and risk management.

Investment Policy
